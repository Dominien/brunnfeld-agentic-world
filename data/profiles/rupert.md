# Rupert

Age: 30. Miner in Brunnfeld, works the Mine.

Rupert has been mining for six years and has formed a steady working partnership with Dieter, who is older and more experienced and from whom Rupert has learned most of what he knows about reading rock and working safely underground. He is saving deliberately toward the purchase of his own set of iron tools — pick, wedge, and hammer — rather than renting them from the communal store, which costs him a portion of every week's wages. The arithmetic is straightforward and motivating: own the tools, keep more of the earnings. He is not ambitious beyond that immediate goal, but achieving it would meaningfully change his financial position and he thinks about it most days he is underground.
