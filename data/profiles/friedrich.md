# Friedrich

Age: 27. Woodcutter in Brunnfeld, works the Forest.

Friedrich is one of the stronger men in Brunnfeld and is not always sure what to do with the energy that produces; he has started more arguments at Liesel's tavern than he has finished, and he is aware enough to be a little ashamed of this. He works alone in the forest most days, which suits his mood better than he admits, and he is good at the work — efficient, fast, not wasteful of usable timber. He sells his wood to Anselm, Wulf, and any household that will pay, and he is starting to realize that splitting and stacking adds more value than he has been charging for. He is not saving for anything specific but feels he should be.
