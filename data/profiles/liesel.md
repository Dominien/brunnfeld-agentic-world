# Liesel

Age: 39. Tavern keeper in Brunnfeld, runs the Tavern.

Liesel runs the only tavern in Brunnfeld and has cultivated a manner that is warm enough to keep people talking and watchful enough to catch everything they say. She extends ale on credit to a small number of regulars and uses that knowledge as social currency more than financial leverage — she is not, primarily, cruel. Her margins depend heavily on market days and the occasional traveling merchant, and a slow winter month forces real economies in how much she fires the hearth. She buys grain for brewing from Hans and Bertram directly, cutting out the mill toll where she can, and she keeps a locked strongbox that is better stocked than most people in the village suspect.
