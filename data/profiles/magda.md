# Magda

Age: 31. Homemaker and market trader in Brunnfeld, Cottage 8.

Magda runs the household on what Dieter earns from the mine, which requires more ingenuity than outsiders appreciate. She is at every market day, buying and occasionally reselling with a precision that has made her quietly profitable at a small scale — she knows the seasonal price of salt, oil, dried fish, and coarse cloth better than most dedicated traders. She worries about Dieter's cough but has learned not to name it aloud because he shuts down the conversation immediately. She has built a small reserve of coin that she has not told him about, not out of dishonesty but out of a considered assessment that someone in the household needs to be prepared for a bad season, and it will not be him.
