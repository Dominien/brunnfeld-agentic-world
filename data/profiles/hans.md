# Hans

Age: 38. Farmer in Brunnfeld, works Farm 1.

Hans has tilled the same fields for nearly two decades and reads the soil the way other men read faces. He owns no land — he pays a share of each harvest to Otto as rent — but he takes quiet pride in yields that outrun his neighbors'. His chief worry is the mill fee Gerda charges; a bad harvest year means the milling costs more than he can spare, and he has borrowed grain before and hated every day of the debt. He is saving slowly toward a second iron plowshare, since the one he has is cracked along the heel and will not last another spring.
