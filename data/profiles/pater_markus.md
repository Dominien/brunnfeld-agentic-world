# Pater Markus

Age: 55. Priest in Brunnfeld, serves the Church.

Pater Markus has been in Brunnfeld long enough that his faith has been worn smooth by contact with real lives — he knows that the dying want comfort more than doctrine, and he provides it without reservation. He is politically careful, maintaining good relations with Otto without becoming his instrument, and he uses his position at deathbeds and confessionals to counsel patience and forgiveness in ways that also, incidentally, keep the village from fracturing. The church's upkeep is a constant financial concern he addresses through tithes and the occasional donation from travelers; he is scrupulous about the books and would sooner go without new vestments than ask more from the poor. He visits Bertha weekly and brings food.
