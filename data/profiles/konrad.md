# Konrad

Age: 45. Cattle farmer in Brunnfeld, Farm 2.

Konrad owns the village herd outright — twelve head of cattle and a few milk cows — which makes him one of the more substantial men in Brunnfeld, though he would not call himself comfortable. Feed costs through a hard winter can wipe out a year's profit from hides and dairy, and he keeps a careful tally of who owes him for draught animals rented during plowing season. He is not unkind, but he does not extend credit a second time to the same man, and he expects the terms of any deal to be honored to the letter. His wife died seven years ago and he has not remarried.
