# Elke

Age: 36. Seamstress in Brunnfeld, works the Seamstress Cottage.

Elke learned her trade from her mother and has improved on it; her stitching is tight and even, and she is one of the few people in Brunnfeld who can produce a garment that will last years rather than seasons. She sources her wool from Konrad and buys linen from traveling merchants on market day, and she resells both finished garments and raw cloth depending on what the season demands. Her prices are fair but not low, which some customers resent and others understand. She keeps a careful stock list and has a good instinct for when to buy ahead before prices rise; this discipline has given her a more stable household than many of her neighbors enjoy.
