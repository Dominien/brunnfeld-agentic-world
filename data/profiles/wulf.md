# Wulf

Age: 37. Carpenter in Brunnfeld, works the Carpenter Shop.

Wulf takes longer than any client wants and produces better work than they expect, and he has made peace with the fact that this is not always commercially ideal. He sources his timber from Friedrich and has a standing arrangement that suits both of them, though Wulf often wishes Friedrich would split the planks straighter before delivery. He repairs carts, builds furniture, fits door frames, and makes the handles for Volker's tools; the two men have a productive if occasionally tense working relationship over specifications. His current private project is a new grain chest for his own cottage, which he has been building from offcuts for two years and is nearly done.
