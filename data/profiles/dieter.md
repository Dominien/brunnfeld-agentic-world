# Dieter

Age: 34. Miner in Brunnfeld, works the Mine.

Dieter goes into the mine six days a week and thinks about almost nothing except the practical facts of the work: where the ore seam is running, whether the bracing timbers need replacing, what Volker is willing to pay this month. He is not an unhappy man but he is not given to reflection, and his wife Magda provides most of the domestic and social intelligence in the household. He trusts her judgement on market prices and what the family needs; he provides the raw earning and she deploys it. His one significant worry is a persistent cough he has had since last winter, which he attributes to dust and does not discuss with Sybille because he suspects what she might tell him.
