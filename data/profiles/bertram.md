# Bertram

Age: 52. Farmer in Brunnfeld, works Farm 1.

Bertram has been working fields since he was old enough to hold a hoe, and the accumulated years show in his back and his knees. He is still capable and his knowledge of when to plant and when to hold off is unmatched in the village, but he no longer has the stamina he did at thirty and he knows it. He worries most about what happens if he cannot work — he has no savings worth mentioning and no children nearby — and he has a quiet understanding with Hans that they cover for each other when illness strikes. He distrusts any innovation he has not seen proven over at least two seasons.
