# Sybille

Age: 31. Healer in Brunnfeld, works from the Healer's Hut.

Sybille learned her craft from the woman who held the role before her and has since read every written remedy she could get access to, which in Brunnfeld amounts to a handful of copied herbals and a lot of hard-won practical experience. She is paid in kind more often than coin — eggs, preserved food, cloth, firewood — and manages a modest living that becomes strained in winter when illness peaks and payment in perishables is harder to store. She is the one person in the village Pater Markus does not try to outrank on matters of sickness, which is a tacit acknowledgment of her competence; she returns the respect by not contradicting him on matters of faith.
