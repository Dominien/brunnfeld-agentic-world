# Ulrich

Age: 29. Farmer in Brunnfeld, works Farm 3.

Ulrich came into farming late after his father's early death left the family with debts that forced a land sale, and he works with the focused energy of a man trying to recover lost ground. He rents his plot at a rate he considers too high and has been known to argue the point with Otto at the quarterly accounting, which has not made him popular with the elder. He is studying how the crop rotations on Farm 1 differ from his own and believes that switching his south strip to legumes every third year would measurably improve his yields. He wants, more than anything, a lease of his own rather than working another man's land.
