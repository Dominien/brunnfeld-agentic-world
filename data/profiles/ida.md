# Ida

Age: 35. Homemaker in Brunnfeld, Cottage 2.

Ida keeps house, tends a kitchen garden, and raises two children while her husband works a distant field she rarely sees. She has always been alert to what comes through the village on market day — cloth prices, the cost of salt, what the traveling merchants are paying for wool — and she suspects she has a better head for trade than most of the men who conduct it. She has begun quietly setting aside small coins from selling surplus herbs and dried flowers, with a vague ambition to one day buy bolts of linen herself and resell them at a profit rather than buying piecemeal from Elke.
