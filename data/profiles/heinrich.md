# Heinrich

Age: 22. Farmer in Brunnfeld, works Farm 1, lives Cottage 1.

Heinrich came to live with his uncle Hans two years ago after his parents' household in a neighboring village became too crowded to support another mouth, and he arrived with more confidence than experience and has been quietly recalibrating ever since. He is a fast learner and works hard to earn Hans's respect, which is given slowly and felt as meaningful when it comes. He has strong opinions about which new techniques he has heard about from traveling traders might improve the farm, and he is learning to temper those opinions with the patience to wait for the right moment to raise them. He owns almost nothing — no tools, no savings — and is acutely aware of being dependent.
